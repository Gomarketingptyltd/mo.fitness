About the Gym

A gym that feels organised, usable, and worth coming back to.

Mo Fitness Club / Strength, classes, and membership that make sense

Mo Fitness Club is designed for people who want a real gym routine, not a burst of motivation that fades in two weeks. Members come here for clear equipment zones, useful classes, sensible programming, and staff who coach instead of hovering.

Whether someone wants open gym access, regular coached sessions, or a stronger weekly routine, the goal is the same: make training simple to follow and easy to stick with.

Facility 01 Free weights, racks, sled, cable stations
Facility 02 Showers, lockers, and easy pre-work sessions
Facility 03 Small-group coaching without overcrowded classes

What members actually care about

  • Clean floor layout and enough equipment to train properly
  • Clear induction for new members and first-time lifters
  • Useful class times before work, after work, and on weekends
  • Coaches who can help with fat loss, strength, and better technique

Classes & Training

Three realistic ways members train here each week.

01 / Access

Open Gym Access

For members who want a clean, well-equipped space to train on their own schedule without crowd-heavy chain gym chaos.

  • Weekday access from 5:00am to 10:00pm
  • Weekend access from 7:00am to 6:00pm
  • Intro induction included

02 / Classes

Coached Strength Classes

Small coached sessions built around proper lifting, conditioning, and weekly consistency rather than random circuits.

  • Morning, lunch, and after-work class times
  • Technique coaching on every session
  • Beginner-friendly options in each block

03 / Coaching

Small Group Coaching

For members who want more structure, closer accountability, and a coach tracking progress across the whole month.

  • Up to 4 clients per session
  • Monthly progression and check-ins
  • Body composition and strength tracking
